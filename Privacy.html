LetterHunter Privacy Policy
This privacy policy describes how Rean&Play Studio collects, uses, and shares information about users of the LetterHunter mobile game application.
Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal identifiable information about LetterHunter users. The game can be downloaded and played anonymously without creating an account. The only information we collect relates to your use of the application, such as gameplay metrics, usage statistics, aggregated analytics, and bug reports. These do not identify individual users.
Use of Information
The usage statistics and metrics we gather are used to improve the LetterHunter game experience. We analyze aggregated user behavior and app performance to optimize gameplay, fix bugs and errors, and better understand user needs. Information may be shared internally or with third party analytics services to assist with improving the application. All data shared is anonymized and does not contain personal identifiable information. We will never sell personal information to third parties or marketers.
Children’s Privacy
LetterHunter does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The game is intended for children ages 6-10 to have fun while learning. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us information without parental consent, it will be immediately deleted. We encourage parents to instruct their children to never provide personal information online without their permission.
Changes to this Policy
We may occasionally make changes to this privacy policy as we add new features and functionality to LetterHunter. We recommend checking back periodically for updates.
